• How to play
  • This is a Memory Game, it's all about memorizing! Look at the images and try to find them inside the board. There is a set limit of 32 clicks to select the correct images.
  • Once you find all the matched images, you won! But remember, if you can't match the images in under 32 trys, you LOSE!
  • Controls
    LEFT CLICK (show the card that you select)
